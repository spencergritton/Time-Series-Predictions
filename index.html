Evaluation of the efficacy of popular recurrent neural network architectures in time-series predictions

Spencer Gritton

Project Information

The idea for this project is to explore popular recurrent neural network architectures and their efficacy in making predictions on time-series financial data (Stocks, Cryptocurrencies, Bonds, etc). Recurrent neural nets are used on a wide variety of problem sets including: Autonomous driving, speech recognition/generation, and many others. The use cases of recurrent neural nets, as well as their great strides in research as of late are the primary fuel in my interest in this topic and I hope to learn a lot about the nuances of this particular type of deep learning.
